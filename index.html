By Carolina Silva

I can feel the happiness, the good memories, the faliers and startovers, each time stronger and stronger. It's here, everything is here an entire life, a path made almost without notice. It's here the restless look, the desiere for answers, that take the art for a moment to leave us its deepest esences.

Dorotea Collection is a ceramic artwork created by the spanish artist Carolina Silva. Handpaint, handmade, nature-inspired are some of the greatest qualities of this dinnerware. They are also secure to use on micowaves and dishwashers so, no excuses, this is a great gift for someone special, someone like you!

handmade&handpaint

The overwhelming pasion for a handmade work is just appreciated for a creative soul. Translating its being's escence with each pattern, showing with this masterpiece the beauty of humanity. Creating magic, finding ways to express itself Developing the hidden ability of feeling creators.

Each piece of Dorotea Collection is made carefully by hand; giving it a deep conexion with the artist and making each piece something special. The mixin of vibrant and seren colors give us a feel of fantastic fusion and showing us a relationship with the diverse. Carolina (the artist) also call the attention on the slight imperfections in her artwork due to her direct interaction with her art which gives a senses of uniqueness to the dinnerware.
